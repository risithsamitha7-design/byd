Our Story

Founded in 2018, Brew & Bean started as a small dream — to create a space where people could slow down, savor exceptional coffee, and connect with one another.

We source our beans from sustainable farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Every batch is carefully roasted in-house to bring out the unique character of each origin.
