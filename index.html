Legacy of GT-R

The GT-R legend began in 1969 with the Skyline GT-R. But the modern era started in 2007 when Nissan unleashed the R35 GT-R upon the world, shocking supercar manufacturers with its blistering performance and relative affordability.

Dubbed "Godzilla" by Australian motoring journalists, the GT-R has dominated tracks worldwide, humbling exotics that cost twice its price.

With its advanced ATTESA E-TS Pro all-wheel drive system, revolutionary damping, and a twin-turbo V6 that produces staggering power, the GT-R remains a benchmark for all-around performance.
